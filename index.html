ViewFinder — Embedded Systems Star Map Project

Embedded systems star map project utilizing the Arduino Uno Q. Via the external Modulino Movement gyroscope + accelerometer, allows users to physically interact and learn more about the cosmos.

Built over the 36 hour IrvineHacks 2026 Hackathon time period.

GitHub | Devpost
MediCheck — AI-Integrated Bill Discrepancy Detector

AI-powered web app for detecting errors in medical bills. Utilizing Gemini API, highlighted potential errors in bills, assigned a certainty score, and drafted an email to be sent to medical providers.

2nd place overall winner at UCI campus wide hackathon — WebJam 2025.

GitHub | Devpost
Debbie — 1 Pound "Antweight" PLA Combat Robot

1 pound PLA Filament vertical-spinner combat robot built in collaboration with Anteater Combat Engineering @ UCI. Designed over two quarters and competed at the UCI Antweight Assault Competition, going head to head against teams from campuses throughout Southern California.
